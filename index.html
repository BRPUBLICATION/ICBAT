About ICBAT 2025

International Conference on Biological Sciences and Advanced Technologies

The International Conference on Biological Sciences and Advanced Technologies (ICBAT 2025) brings together visionary minds from academia and industry to explore the latest breakthroughs and innovations shaping the future of life sciences and technology.

This interdisciplinary platform will delve into diverse topics such as biotechnology, genomics, biomedical engineering, environmental sciences, nanotechnology, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and synthetic biology — highlighting their impact on research, healthcare, and sustainability.

As a Scopus-indexed event, ICBAT 2025 offers invaluable opportunities for networking, collaboration, and the exchange of pioneering ideas among global scientists, researchers, and professionals.

Biotechnology and Society

Exploring the ethical, social, and economic impacts of biotechnology.

Research on infectious diseases and strategies for global health improvement.
